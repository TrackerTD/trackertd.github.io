    • The Sonic Perfect System Collection is the definitive way to experience the Sonic the Hedgehog series.

    Ideal for beginners and longtime fans alike, the Perfect System Collection offers an easy to follow guide to obtaining the ideal versions of over 30 years worth of Sonic games. Inside are recommendations for a slew of mods, quality of life tweaks, custom ports, and more. You'll also find 'Bonus Game' recommendations, covering games with a singular overhaul mod, and fangames on par with the 'real' stuff.

    If you're reading this, you're early! The Perfect System guide launches October 16th!



    This guide is best viewed in Adobe Acrobat, Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. Firefox does not currently support some of the layer effects used.

    The Perfect System guide is available in two versions: a perfomance version and full-scale version. The full-scale version may be slow to load on some PCs, and so the performance version offers slight image compression to combat this.
